About us

JamCoffee Holdings Limited is a privately funded investor focusing on long-term investments in the Jamaican Specialty Coffee Segment.

Our areas of activities are:

1
Equity Investments
2
Active participation as shareholder in portfolio companies
3
Consulting projects (strategy, sales and marketing)
4
and M&A for portfolio companies

JamCoffee Holdings target companies in the following segments of the Jamaican Specialty Coffee Business:

1
Coffee Growers
2
Coffee Works Facilities
3
Coffee Roasters
4
Coffee Exporters
5
Other coffee related projects

Mission

JamCoffee Holdings is targeting coffee dealers, coffee works facilities and operators focusing on coffee products.
Our projects will focus on equity investments structured financing and active participation in the development of its portfolio companies.

Vision

JamCoffee Holdings aims to be the key privately funded investor for the Jamaican Specialty Coffee segment.
JamCoffee Holdings is determined to be a trusted advisor, engaging with all its clients on a partnership level.
